Algorithm to Code

Embark on a solo mission to transform abstract algorithms into elegant code. Like navigating through a wormhole, you'll need to precisely translate theoretical concepts into practical solutions. Your code will be judged on accuracy, efficiency, and adherence to programming best practices.

Designathon

Like crafting a new world, you'll have 1.5 hours to design a complete UI/UX solution. Your mission: create an intuitive interface that bridges human interaction with digital technology. Use any design tool of your choice to bring your vision to life, then present your creation to our expert panel.

Register

Tech Maze

Navigate through three challenging dimensions as a team of three explorers. From memorizing technical knowledge in Byte Bash to reconstructing code in Sort the Stash, and finally proving your mettle in Code Bee. Like traversing through different galaxies, each round presents unique challenges that test different aspects of your technical prowess.

Mission Phases:

  • Round 1: Byte Bash - Technical Memory Challenge
  • Round 2: Sort the Stash - Code Assembly Mission
  • Round 3: Code Bee - Real-time Output Analysis

Devxtreme Hackathon

The ultimate mission: a one-day expedition into innovation and entrepreneurship. Like planning an interstellar journey, teams will develop both technical solutions and viable business strategies. Your mission culminates in a pitch to our expert panel, showcasing your prototype and its potential impact.

Mission Timeline:

  • 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM: Ideation & Planning Phase
  • 12:30 PM - 3:30 PM: Prototype Development
  • 4:00 PM - 4:45 PM: Final Pitch & Evaluation
